Pasta

Pasta is a traditional Italian cuisine staple made from durum wheat, water, and sometimes eggs, then formed into various shapes and sizes. It's a versatile dish that can be paired with a variety of sauces, vegetables, meats, and cheeses, making it a popular choice worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Durum wheat pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook the pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic until golden.
  3. Add tomato sauce and season with salt, black pepper, and basil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce until well coated.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.
  6. Enjoy your delicious homemade pasta!
